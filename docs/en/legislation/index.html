Draft law text placeholder

Article 1 — Purpose. The law creates the Société à Responsabilité Limitée (SARL), a commercial company form with legal personality.

Article 2 — Limited liability. Shareholder liability is limited to each shareholder’s contribution.

Article 3 — Name and registered office. The company operates under the SARL designation and a registered office in Haiti.
